Proxy

($10.00/month)

Peer-to-peer app-based group messaging with proxy/pooled numbers. Supporting personalized services and non-exposure of personal numbers for enterprise or A2P communications. A phone number is assigned to a conversation (e.g. end-user/agent pair), rather than to a single business or individual. Both parties to the conversation send text messages to that number, which acts as a relay and protects the privacy of both users' personal numbers.
