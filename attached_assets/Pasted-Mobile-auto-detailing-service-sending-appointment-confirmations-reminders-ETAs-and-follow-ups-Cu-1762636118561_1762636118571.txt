How do end-users consent to receive messages?
Customers consent through our online booking system at https://cleanmachinetulsa.com/sms-consent When booking an appointment, customers must check a consent checkbox stating: "I Consent to Receive Informational messages & Alerts from Clean Machine Auto Detail. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time." The consent form includes: - Explicit checkbox with opt-in language - Phone number field where customers enter their contact information - Timestamp and IP address logging for audit compliance - Clear STOP opt-out instructions All messages include STOP opt-out instructions. We never send unsolicited marketing to numbers that only consented to appointment communications. Privacy Policy: https://cleanmachinetulsa.com/privacy-policy
