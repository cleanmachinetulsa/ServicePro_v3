## Overview

Clean Machine Auto Detail uses a comprehensive digital platform to manage all aspects of the business. This guide will help new employees understand how to use the system effectively.

---

## Employee Roles

### Available Roles:
1. **Owner** - Full system access, business management
2. **Manager** - Administrative access, employee management, scheduling
3. **Technician** - Field operations, job management, customer communication
4. **Employee** - Basic access for support staff

---

## Onboarding Process

### Phase 1: Application & Hiring
1. **Job Application** (Public-facing at `/careers`)
   - Submit application with resume
   - Provide contact information
   - Answer screening questions

2. **Application Review** (Admin at `/admin-applications`)
   - Manager reviews application
   - Status: New → Screening → Interviewing → Rejected/Hired

3. **Account Creation** (Admin at `/user-management`)
   - Manager creates employee account
   - Assigns role (Technician, Employee, Manager)
   - Sets up login credentials

### Phase 2: Profile Setup
1. **First Login**
   - Change temporary password at `/change-password`
   - Set up profile at `/tech-profile`

2. **Complete Bio Profile** (For Technicians)
   - Upload professional photo
   - Write bio (AI assistance available via `/tech-wizard`)
   - Add specialties and certifications
   - Manager reviews and approves profile

### Phase 3: Training & Orientation
1. **System Navigation**
   - Dashboard overview
   - Communication tools
   - Schedule management
   - Job workflows

2. **Safety & Procedures**
   - Vehicle handling protocols
   - Chemical safety
   - Customer interaction guidelines
   - Quality standards

## Training Resources

### For Technicians

#### 1. **Tech Wizard** (`/tech-wizard`)
- AI-powered bio writing assistant
- Helps create professional, trust-building bios
- Suggests specialties and tags
- Preview how customers see your profile

#### 2. **Daily Workflow**
**Morning:**
- Check schedule at `/technician-schedule`
- Review job details for the day
- Check weather forecasts
- Ensure supplies are stocked

**During Jobs:**
- Navigate to customer location (GPS integration)
- Update job status to "On Site" when arriving
- Take before/after photos
- Collect payment if cash job
- Update job status to "Completed"

**End of Day:**
- Submit daily deposit summary
- Review tomorrow's schedule
- Check for new messages

#### 3. **Communication Hub**
- View all customer conversations at `/messages`
- Respond to customer questions
- Escalate issues to management if needed

### For Managers

#### 1. **Employee Management** (`/admin-employees`)
- View all employee profiles
- Approve bio submissions
- Request profile changes
- Monitor employee activity

#### 2. **Scheduling** (`/scheduling-dashboard`)
- Weekly calendar grid view
- 30-day technician schedules
- Assign shifts using templates
- Manage conflicts and overtime

#### 3. **PTO Management** (`/admin-pto`)
- Review PTO requests
- Approve/deny with reasons
- Track PTO balances
- Monitor coverage gaps

## Daily Operations

### Technician Dashboard (`/technician`)

**Four Main Pods:**

1. **Schedule Panel** (Left)
   - Today's appointments with times
   - Customer names and addresses
   - Service types
   - Click for navigation

2. **Workflow Pod** (Top Right)
   - Update job status
   - Upload photos
   - Add notes
   - Mark completed

3. **Communications Pod** (Middle Right)
   - Recent conversations
   - Unread message count
   - Quick reply to customers
   - SMS notifications

4. **Media Pod** (Bottom Right)
   - Upload before/after photos
   - Link photos to specific jobs
   - Preview uploaded images

### Schedule Management

#### Viewing Your Schedule
- **30-Day View:** `/technician-schedule`
- **Shows:**
  - All your assigned shifts
  - Shift times (e.g., 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
  - Shift templates (Opening, Closing, Mid-Shift)
  - PTO/time off
  - Open shifts available to claim

#### Requesting Time Off
1. Go to `/request-pto`
2. Select date range
3. Choose PTO type (Vacation, Sick, Personal, etc.)
4. Add reason/notes
5. Submit for approval
6. Manager reviews and responds

#### Shift Trading
1. Go to `/shift-trades`
2. Select shift you want to trade
3. Choose technician to trade with
4. Submit trade request
5. Both parties and manager must approve
6. System validates no conflicts/overtime

## Common Tasks & How-To's

### How to Update Job Status
1. Open Technician Dashboard (`/technician`)
2. Find job in Schedule Panel
3. Click on job card
4. Use Workflow Pod dropdown
5. Select new status
6. Add notes if needed
7. Click "Update Status"

### How to Upload Photos
1. Click on job in Workflow Pod
2. Scroll to Media Pod
3. Click "Upload Photos"
4. Select images from device
5. Photos auto-link to current job
6. Add captions (optional)
7. Submit

### How to Respond to Customers
1. Go to Messages (`/messages`)
2. Click customer conversation
3. Type reply in text box
4. Click Send or press Enter
5. Message sent via SMS to customer

### How to Request a Shift Trade
1. Go to Shift Trades (`/shift-trades`)
2. Click "Request Trade"
3. Select your shift to trade
4. Choose who to trade with
5. Add message explaining why
6. Submit request
7. Wait for approval from peer and manager

### How to Check PTO Balance
1. Go to Request PTO (`/request-pto`)
2. Balance shown at top of page
3. View pending requests
4. See approved/denied history

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In
- Verify username/password with manager
- Use "Forgot Password" link if needed
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Contact manager if still failing

### Photos Won't Upload
- Check internet connection
- File size must be under 10MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, HEIC
- Try reducing photo quality in camera settings

### Schedule Not Showing
- Refresh page
- Check if shift was assigned (ask manager)
- Verify you're logged in as correct user
- Clear browser cache

### Customer Not Responding
- Check message was sent (green checkmark)
- Verify customer phone number is correct
- Try calling customer directly
- Escalate to manager if urgent

## Best Practices

### Communication
✅ Respond to customer messages within 1 hour  
✅ Use professional, friendly tone  
✅ Confirm details before jobs (time, address, services)  
✅ Update customers on delays immediately  
❌ Don't ignore messages or leave on "read"  
❌ Don't share personal contact info  

### Photography
✅ Take before AND after photos of every job  
✅ Show problem areas in detail  
✅ Capture full vehicle angles  
✅ Upload same day as job completion  
❌ Don't forget to take photos (required)  
❌ Don't upload blurry or dark images  

### Professionalism
✅ Arrive on time (call if running late)  
✅ Dress in uniform  
✅ Park considerately at customer locations  
✅ Clean up work area completely  
❌ Don't smoke/vape near customer property  
❌ Don't use customer facilities without permission
